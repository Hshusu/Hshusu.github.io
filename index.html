Food: A Taste Odessey
From Alfredo to Zeppole food comes in many shapes and sizes, yet one thing is consistent throughout : they are all italian.. wait no thats wrong ! they provide sustinence and flavour for people all over the world. For all of human history food has played a historical role in many historical events. So instead of focusing on human history, let us focus on the history of food
~4000BC
Though humans consumed food before this period, the ancient egyptians were found to have been the first to create food using simple recipies such as leavened bread
~1st Century
Though fried chicken is widely known as a relatively modern dish, it actually is one of the most enduring dishes throughout time and culture , having outlasted its cotemporaries such as challah and Blancmange
~10th century
Sharkfin soup was invented by chinese sailors sailing on the straits of malaya . Rip many sharks

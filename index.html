Ruined SRPG
A card based Strategy role playing game.The player controls a diverse roster of characters with cards that they may use to defend, attack or clash with. Cards have dice which are rolled against opposing cards with the higher rolling dice winning. The game focuses on using character passives, stacking buffs and being able to defend incoming attacks to build on the strategy rpg genre.

Hike.
Client project for the Education and Career Guidance Counselors to develop a mobile game to introduce polytechnic life to Year 1 students during their first Personal Career Strategy lesson.
Take a short hike up a mountain and reach its peak, representing the trials and tribulations of student life. Encounter life events and ‘enemies’ that challenge your way, & make decisions that shape your student life, career path and outcome. Learn that the way up is not always a straight line, and see the unique route you took, along with the different skills/hats that you accumulated.
At the end of the game, players will be able to see a summary of their unique route up the mountain, along with the different skills/hats that they accumulated, and how they compare to their peers.

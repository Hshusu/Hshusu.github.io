About Me:
I'm an aspiring Game Developer and Designer working across both programming and game design. I code in C#, C++, JavaScript, and Python, with experience in Unity, Unreal, and Godot. As a designer, I’ve led multiple game jams, developing experimental concepts and crafting engaging gameplay systems for my projects. I’m also passionate about digital art, working on character design and 2D and 3D animation.
My Works
Key Projects
Ruined SRPG
A card based Strategy role playing game.The player controls a diverse roster of characters with cards that they may use to defend, attack or clash with. Cards have dice which are rolled against opposing cards with the higher rolling dice winning. The game focuses on using character passives, stacking buffs and being able to defend incoming attacks to build on the strategy rpg genre.
